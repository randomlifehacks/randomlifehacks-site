FAQ


Will there be more outfits/backgrounds?

New outfits will be uploaded every few days. Feel free to email me at randomlifehacksreq@gmail.com for requests :)

How do I use the printable files?

Simply hit the download link below the image you want, print, and enjoy!

What size should I print things?

As long as you print them all at the same size, it doesn't truly matter. I prefer a5 size at 110% ratio.

Why did you make Dress Bean?

First - this section will be long as Google prefers websites with over 600 words.

I created Bean as a way to pass time. I love drawing, arts and crafts, anything that keeps my hands busy.

When I first created Bean, it was really a self project. Whenever I'd get bored, I'd doodle a new outfit for Bean.

Every time I made a new background or new outfit, I'd send it to my friends and they'd keep telling me that I should make "Dress Bean" a business.

I however, refuse to lol. Bean is free for me to draw and print. That was kind of the appeal of it.

I tend to bounce from hobby to hobby, but with Bean, I bounce from outfit to outfit which saves money. It didn't feel right to charge other people for it.

Now Dress Bean is a part of my Random Life Hacks website so my friends print Bean and the accesories for their kids to play with!
