Dress Bean – Free Printable Paper Doll

Meet Bean! Dress Bean is a free printable paper doll craft created as a relaxing, creative activity. Download Bean’s clothes and backgrounds, print them at home, and cut them out to dress Bean however you like.

Bean is on the swimsuits page.

This project is great for people of all ages who enjoy arts and crafts, paper dolls, cozy activities, caring for little characters, or people who are just bored.

Frequently Asked Questions

Will there be more outfits/backgrounds?

New outfits will be uploaded every few days. Feel free to email me at randomlifehacksreq@gmail.com for requests :)

How do I use the printable files?

Simply hit the download link below the image you want, print, and enjoy!

What size should I print things?

As long as you print them all at the same size, it doesn't truly matter. I prefer a5 size at 110% ratio.
