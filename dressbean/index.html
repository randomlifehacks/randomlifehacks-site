Dress Bean – Free Printable Paper Doll

Meet Bean! Dress Bean is a free printable paper doll craft created as a relaxing, creative activity. Download Bean’s clothes and backgrounds, print them at home, and cut them out to dress Bean however you like.

Bean is on the swimsuits page.

All printable outfits are designed to fit Bean perfectly. This project is great for kids, teens, and adults who enjoy arts and crafts, paper dolls, cozy activities, or caring for little characters.

I recommend printing everything at 110% in A5 format, but feel free to use whatever size works best for you.

✏️ New printable outfits and backgrounds are added every few days.
